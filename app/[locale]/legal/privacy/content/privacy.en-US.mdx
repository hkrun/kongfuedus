## 1. Information Collection and Use

### (1) Registration and Account Information

When you register and create a Kong Fu Master account, we may collect the following information to complete the account creation process and identify your account:

1. **Basic Identity Information**: Such as your name, phone number, and email address (if you register using email, including zjhkrun@gmail.com or any other email address you provide).

2. **Account Settings Information**: Such as your username and password (we encrypt your password to ensure account security).

This information will be used to: provide you with account login services, identify your account, assist you in recovering your account password, and send you account-related notifications (such as account security reminders and course update notifications).

### (2) Information Collected During Service Use

1. **Learning Behavior Information**: When you use our online martial arts video course services, we collect your learning records, including the courses you watch, viewing duration, viewing progress, completed course assignments, and test results. This information will be used to provide you with personalized learning recommendations, track your learning progress, and help us optimize course content and teaching service quality.

2. **Device and Technical Information**: To ensure normal use of our services and improve service stability and security, we may collect information about the devices you use, including device model, operating system version, browser type, device unique identifiers (such as IMEI, MAC address, etc.), IP address, network access method and network status (such as Wi-Fi, 4G/5G, etc.), and access date and time.

3. **Payment Information**: When you purchase our courses (such as children's martial arts basic training courses, strangle escape technique courses, etc.) or subscribe to our monthly membership ($39.99 per month, with unlimited access to 100+ courses and other benefits), you need to provide payment information, including your chosen payment method (such as credit card information, third-party payment account information, etc.). We only collect payment information necessary to complete the payment transaction and transmit such information to third-party payment service providers to complete the payment process. We do not store your complete payment card information or other sensitive payment data.

### (3) User Feedback and Communication Information

When you contact us through website messages, email (such as zjhkrun@gmail.com), WhatsApp, or other means to provide feedback, inquire about services, or make suggestions, we will collect relevant information you provide during communication, including your communication content and contact information, so that we can respond to your inquiries and handle your issues in a timely manner.

## 2. Information Storage and Protection

### (1) Information Storage

1. **Storage Location**: We store your personal information on servers that comply with legal requirements. These servers may be located within or outside China (if cross-border storage is involved, we will ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations on cross-border data transmission and take necessary security measures).

2. **Storage Period**: We will store your personal information for the period necessary to achieve the purposes of information collection in accordance with legal requirements and the provisions of this policy. When the storage period expires or the purposes of information collection are no longer necessary, we will promptly delete your personal information or anonymize it (unless otherwise required by law). For example, after your account is cancelled, we will delete your account-related information within a reasonable period, but may retain some necessary information to comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, etc.

### (2) Information Protection

We attach great importance to the security of your personal information and have adopted a series of reasonable and feasible technical and management measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, modification, damage, or loss, including:

1. **Technical Safeguards**: We use encryption technology (such as SSL/TLS encryption) to protect your personal information during transmission; use access control technology to limit access to personal information, only authorizing necessary staff to access relevant information when performing their duties; regularly conduct security inspections and maintenance of systems, and promptly repair potential security vulnerabilities.

2. **Management Safeguards**: We establish and improve information security management systems, clarify the responsibilities and authorities of various departments and staff in the personal information processing process; conduct regular privacy protection and information security training for staff to improve their privacy protection awareness and safe operation capabilities; develop emergency plans to take timely measures for handling personal information security incidents and report to relevant departments and notify you in accordance with legal requirements.

Although we have taken the above security measures, please understand that due to the complexity and openness of internet technology and the uncertainty of the network environment, we cannot completely guarantee the absolute security of your personal information. You should properly keep your account information (such as username and password), avoid disclosing account information to others, and pay special attention to protecting personal information security when using public devices or networks. If you find that your account information is being used illegally or there is a risk of personal information leakage, please contact us immediately (contact information is provided in Section 7 of this policy), and we will assist you in taking appropriate remedial measures.

1. We will strictly use your personal information for the purposes specified in Section 1 of this policy. ## 5. User Rights

In the course of using our services, you have the following rights regarding personal information in accordance with the law, and we will provide necessary assistance for you to exercise these rights:

1. **Right to Access and Query**: You have the right to access your personal information, including basic information provided when registering your account and learning behavior information. 2. **Right to Modify and Correct**: If you find that your personal information is incorrect or incomplete, you have the right to request us to modify or correct it. You can modify some editable information through the editing function in your account. For information that cannot be modified by yourself, you can contact us through the contact information specified in Section 7 of this policy, and we will process it in a timely manner after verifying your identity.

3. **Right to Delete**: You have the right to request us to delete your personal information when it complies with legal requirements and one of the following circumstances:

   (1) Our collection or use of your personal information violates legal requirements or exceeds the scope of purposes specified in this policy;

   (2) You no longer use our services and request deletion of your personal information;

   (3) We stop providing relevant services and there is no need to continue retaining your personal information.

   You can submit a deletion request by contacting us. We will verify your identity and the reason for the request, process it within a reasonable period, and inform you of the processing result.

4. **Right to Cancel Account**: You have the right to cancel your Kong Fu Master account. You can cancel your account through the cancellation function in your account or by contacting us. After account cancellation, we will delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with Section 2 of this policy (unless otherwise required by law). Please note that after account cancellation, you will no longer be able to use the account to log in and use our services, and relevant rights in the account (such as purchased courses that have not expired, membership benefits, etc.) may be affected. Please proceed with caution.

5. **Right to Withdraw Consent**: You have the right to withdraw your consent to the processing of personal information. For example, you can withdraw your consent to our sending you marketing information (you can complete the withdrawal through the unsubscribe function in the information or by contacting us). Please note that after withdrawing consent, we will no longer process your relevant personal information based on that consent, but this will not affect our lawful processing of personal information based on your consent before you withdrew it.
